FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions.

How can I receive QRL in the wallet?

+

To receive QRL, tap on the "Receive" button on the main screen. This will display your QRL address and a QR code. Share this address with the sender, or let them scan the QR code to transfer QRL to your wallet.

How do I send QRL to another user?

+

To send QRL, tap on the "Send" button. Enter the recipient's QRL address (or scan their QR code), input the amount you wish to send, and confirm the transaction. The app will ask for your confirmation before finalizing the transfer.

Are messages sent through the blockchain private?

+

No, messages sent through the blockchain messaging feature are public and can be viewed by anyone with access to the QRL blockchain. Do not include sensitive or private information in these messages.

What happens if I lose access to my wallet?

+

When you create a wallet, you are provided with a hexseed and mnemonic phrase. These are your recovery keys. Store them securely offline. If you lose access to your wallet, you can use either of these to restore your wallet and access your funds.
